Not a consciousness detector

One system.
Eight different questions.

A system can be strongly agentic, weakly persistent, highly self-modeling, and almost unassessed for valence. This ledger preserves that structure instead of hiding it inside “sentient: yes/no.”

The output is conditional. It reports what the selected evidence would support, what it would not prove, the strongest rival explanation, and the next discriminating test.
